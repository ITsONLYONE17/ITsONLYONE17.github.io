Donovan Ramirez

Why hello there, welcome yet again to my website! While humble as it is now, it will surely expand as my work expands and my experience grows. Now, how bout’ I tell you about myself a little?
To reintroduce myself, I’m Donovan Ramirez. I was born on January 17th, 2006, in Boise, Idaho. During my childhood, I’ve always had video games as a great part of it. While during my youth it was simply a way to entertain and have fun, it has grown to something much more than a simple hobby for me. Whether it was Call of Duty with my sister on the Xbox 360, or Pokémon Sapphire with my friends at school, I have always had video games as a point for community in my life. It was an escape from the boring times, a chance for my mind to explore and reimagine what is and isn’t possible.

And from that great love of games, I find myself where I am now: a sophomore in Games, Interactive Media and Mobile (GIMM) at Boise State University. I now work to learn and make the games that have brought me so much joy in the past, and hopefully, in the future, make a career from it!

Some of my favorite games are Elden Ring, various Pokémon titles (especially Sapphire), Hollow Knight, and Dead Cells.

For some more about me, some other hobbies I have are card collecting (Pokemon), listening to music, and working out. Additionally, some friends and I have gotten into DND over the past year, and I have slowly (but surely) been working on a campaign myself!

But I suppose that's enough about me. However, if there’s anything you’d like to ask, feel free to reach out. Don’t be a stranger!
