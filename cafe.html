The Third Circle Cafe

In this group project, we were tasked with creating a video game using our knowledge of Unity/C#, as well as implementing Augmented Reality (AR) into our game. We decided to create a cooking game, where the player would be a chef in a cafe, going through the process of making and delivering a cake to guests. The game was designed to be played on a computer alongside a phone or other mobile device for its camera, intending to use AR to allow players to interact with the game world in a more immersive way.
