The Glowing Cavern

In this project, I was tasked with creating a unreachable location, whether metaphorical or literal. For this, I decided to create a cave full of glowing trees and an abandoned ruins. With multiple inspirations from various games, such as Elden Ring's Underground and Baldur's Gate's Underdark, I decided to create this environment completely devoid of light, oustide of the glowing from the trees and a minor trickle of light from a crack in the ceiling.

This was my first time working in Unreal Engine, and I learned a lot about level design, as well as gaining a better understanding of the 3D modeling and the engine.
